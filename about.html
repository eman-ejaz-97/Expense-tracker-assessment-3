Our Story

From Spreadsheets to Smart Tracking

Founded in early 2025, Smart Expense Tracking System was born from a simple frustration: managing personal and business finances shouldn't require an accounting degree or hours of manual data entry.

Muhammad Eman Ejaz, our Founder and CEO, brought together a team of passionate professionals to turn this vision into reality. Alongside Muhammad Waqas Iqbal, who leads our financial strategy as Chief Financial Officer, and Sajina Rana Magar, who heads our Communications, they created a tool that makes expense tracking intuitive, automated, and actually enjoyable. What started as a side project has grown into a platform trusted by over 50,000 users worldwide.

Today, our team continues to innovate and expand our capabilities, driven by user feedback and our commitment to financial empowerment for all.
